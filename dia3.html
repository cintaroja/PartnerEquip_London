AWS Expert

Best Practices for Conducting Fault Experimentations

Chaos engineering involves intentionally introducing failures into cloud environments to uncover weaknesses and improve system robustness.

Key Principles

  • Starting with small-scale experiments
  • Automating testing processes
  • Continuously refining experiments based on observed outcomes

What You'll Learn

  • How to design and execute controlled experiments that simulate real-world disruptions
  • Analyze the impact on cloud service performance
  • Develop strategies to mitigate potential issues

Benefits

  • Empower your cloud operations teams to build more resilient and reliable workloads
  • Learn the art of chaos engineering
